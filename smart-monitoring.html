Enterprise-Grade Cyber Security

Environmental telemetry data is highly sensitive. JIYAR Insight™ utilizes AES 256-bit data encryption standards for all streaming pipelines.

Our software layer establishes isolated, outbound-only server connections, guaranteeing that your plant's primary PLC, SCADA, or automation networks remain completely hidden and safe from external web threats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will our plant get spammed with false alerts?

No. We uses dual-stage validation filtering. It checks data variance against historical baseline parameters to filter out momentary electrical noise or routine sensor purge cycles before sending an active alert to your phone.

Does this software replace our existing SPCB server connection?

Our smart system works alongside your current setup. It acts as a local data filter and personal dashboard for your internal team while maintaining a clean, uninterrupted parallel data stream to government servers.

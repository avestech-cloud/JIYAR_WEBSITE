Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

At JIYAR Environmental Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("JIYAR", "we", "our", or "us"), securing your industrial data is as critical as ensuring your compliance. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, transmit, and protect data through our PredictX software, field services, and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Corporate Contact Information: Names, designations, email addresses, and phone numbers of plant managers, EHS officers, Site incharge and operators who interact with our services.
  • Industrial Telemetry Data: Real-time stack emissions, ambient air quality metrics, water quality parameters, and analytical instrument health diagnostics captured by our hardware and JIYAR DataSetu gateways.
  • Site Infrastructure Data: Schematics, network configurations, and process parameter thresholds required to properly install and calibrate monitoring equipment.

2. How We Use Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your corporate or telemetry data to marketing agencies. Your data is strictly used for operational excellence:

  • To populate your localized JIYAR Insight™ Cloud Dashboards.
  • To execute predictive maintenance and dispatch field engineers before equipment failure.
  • To alert designated plant personnel of impending compliance breaches via SMS/WhatsApp.

3. Third-Party Sharing & Regulatory Compliance

As an environmental compliance partner, our systems are explicitly designed to interface with government infrastructure. By utilizing our connected CEMS/AAQMS/EQMS systems, you authorize JIYAR to automatically transmit required emission and telemetry data directly to the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) and relevant State Pollution Control Boards (SPCBs) as mandated by Indian environmental laws.

4. Data Security & Encryption

Industrial data integrity is paramount. We employ AES-256 bit encryption for all telemetry pipelines. Our hardware utilizes isolated, outbound-only server handshakes to ensure your plant’s primary SCADA/PLC networks remain secure and isolated from external web threats.

5. Data Retention

We retain your historical telemetry data on secure cloud servers for the duration required to fulfill your active Service Level Agreements (SLAs) or as dictated by statutory environmental audit timelines (typically up to 5 years). Upon contract termination, data is securely archived or purged according to legal requirements.

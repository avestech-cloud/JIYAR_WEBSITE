Our Technology Sourcing & Integration Philosophy

At JIYAR, we operate as an independent system integrator and turnkey solution provider. We do not lock our clients into a single manufacturing line; instead, we source high-precision analytical core components exclusively from trusted global OEMs that carry international USEPA, TUV, and MCERTS certifications.

Our engineering team builds custom-tailored sample conditioning systems, climate-controlled villas, fluidic line networks, and secure Industrial IoT telemetry wrappers around these components. This ensures maximum uptime and flawless data continuity in the harshest industrial conditions across Eastern India.
